Why Poonamallee?

DEVELOPMENT-DRIVEN

Development abounds in Poonamallee with 5 million sq. ft. of proposed Grade A office space, a fully functional airport terminal in 2024 at Thiruvallur, a 25-acre solar-powered bus stop, and the government's upcoming Satellite Township.

APPRECIATING AREA

With a cost advantage of 50%, Poonamallee stands out as an affordable and promising locale for real estate investments; guaranteed to appreciate with proposed developments like Chennai Metro Rail, infrastructure upgrades, and more.

UNMATCHED CONNECTIVITY

Poonamallee is a hyper-connected locale, with easy access to the city, the industrial hubs of Ambattur and Avadi, the IT cluster at Manapakkam, and the Chennai International Airport. Connectivity improves further with the proposed Metro Rail.
